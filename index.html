About Me

I'm a web developer who loves UX/UI design. I bring together my tech skills with a creative background to make user-friendly websites. I'm passionate about crafting clear, easy-to-use web apps that stand out for their innovative design. In web development, I specialize in front-end technologies such as HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and React, striving to create responsive and interactive websites. I integrate modern development practices with a deep understanding of user experience principles. Additionally, I possess practical experience in UX/UI design, holding a Google UX Design Certificate. This enables me to create designs that prioritize user needs and enhance overall user experience.
